What are Hubs?

A Hub is a travel coordination point. It groups campuses within 25 km so one team can cover them in a single trip.

Campuses beyond 25 km from any hub are marked with a 🔷 diamond — they need a curated travel plan to be reached.

Toggle hubs on to see zone boundaries on the map.
